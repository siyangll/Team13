Smart Water Bottle!!

Produced by Team13-Beginners: Kevin Wang, Siyang Li, Fangying Gao

Product Review

Our Smart Water Bottle is designed to monitor your daily water intake, water quality, and heart rate, ensuring both the safety of your drinking water and promoting healthy hydration habits. The bottle can sync data in real-time to your smartphone or computer, allowing you to track these key metrics effortlessly. If you haven't had a drink of water in 30 minutes, the bottle will remind you to hydrate. Interestingly, if you're feeling unwell, you can even use our bottle to quickly measure your heart rate!

Key features include accurate data acquisition, local and remote monitoring, and efficient data storage. This prototype highlights the potential of health-conscious smart devices in improving daily hydration and wellness.

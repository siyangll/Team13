Smart Bottle!!

Produced by Team13-Beginners: Kevin Wang, Siyang Li, Fangying Gao

Product Review

The Smart Bottle project integrates various sensors to monitor water intake, water quality, and heart rate. The Product Review section showcases the assembly of sensors and modules, while the System Diagram on the right details the logical connections among components.

Key features include accurate data acquisition, local and remote monitoring, and efficient data storage. This prototype highlights the potential of health-conscious smart devices in improving daily hydration and wellness.
